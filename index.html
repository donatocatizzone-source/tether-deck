Sustainable Business Model

Safety apps traditionally fail due to high churn: users leave when they aren't actively dating or selling. Tether's hybrid model captures the entire market.

Pay-Per-Mission ($0.99)

Captures the 85% "Occasional User". An impulse buy at the moment of anxiety. High margin, low barrier.

Gig Subscription ($9.99/mo)

For Realtors, Drivers, and Power Sellers. Includes automated tax receipts & unlimited missions.

FUTURE GROWTH

The "Guardian" Model

We monetize the protector's anxiety, not the user's. Parents pay the subscription; the app remains free for the teen.

B2B Trust API

Users build a "Vouch Score" by completing safe meetups. We license this trust badge back to Tinder and Marketplace.
